Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-06

AR Driver Quiz ("the app") is a private study aid for the Arkansas driver knowledge test, used by a single family. This policy explains what the app collects, why, how long it is kept, and how to have it deleted.

What we collect

When you sign in with Google, the app stores, in Google Firebase (Firestore):

  • Your Google account email address.
  • Your Google display name.
  • Your quiz results: attempt records (score, topic, date, duration, which questions were missed), spaced-repetition study state, and a summary (readiness score, streak, per-topic mastery, last exam).

Access is restricted to family members on a manually maintained allowlist. The app collects nothing from anyone not on that list.

Why we collect it

Solely to run the study app and to let the parent/guardian account track each child's progress and readiness for the test. The data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for advertising.

No third-party analytics or advertising

The app uses no third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. The only external service is Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore), which stores the data above so the app can function. See Firebase's privacy documentation.

Data retention

Quiz results are retained while the account remains on the family allowlist so progress persists across devices. The dashboard reads only each account's most recent attempts. Data is retained no longer than needed for progress tracking and is deleted on request (see below). Removing an account from the allowlist stops all further collection for that account.

Deleting or exporting your data

A signed-in user can delete their own account data, and the parent/guardian who administers the family allowlist can remove a child's account and its stored data. To request deletion or a copy of the data held for an account, contact the family administrator (the parent account holder) or email leonard.derek.a@gmail.com. Deletion requests are honored promptly.

Children's privacy

The app is intended for Arkansas learner's-permit students (age 14 and up), used under the supervision of the parent/guardian who administers the account allowlist. It is not directed to children under 13, and no account is created without the administering parent adding it. If you believe a child's data has been collected without appropriate consent, contact us at the address above and it will be deleted.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Material changes will be communicated to family members.
